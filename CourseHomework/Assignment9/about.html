Why Maintain Your Skis?

Regular ski maintenance improves performance, extends the lifespan of your skis, and ensures a smooth ride on the slopes.

FAQs

  • How often should I wax my skis? - After every 5-7 days of skiing.
  • Can I use any iron for waxing? - A ski-specific waxing iron is recommended to avoid overheating.
  • What type of wax should I use? - Choose a wax based on snow temperature and conditions.
