How to Wax and Tune Your Skis

Follow these steps to keep your skis in top condition.

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

You'll need ski wax, an iron, a plastic scraper, a nylon brush, and a workbench with ski clamps.

Step 2: Clean the Base

Use a base cleaner or a citrus-based solvent to remove dirt and old wax from the ski base.

Step 3: Apply Wax

Heat the ski wax with an iron and drip it evenly across the ski base.

Step 4: Spread the Wax

Use the iron to spread the wax evenly, moving it slowly to prevent burning the base.

Step 5: Let the Wax Cool

Allow the wax to set for at least 30 minutes to fully absorb into the base.

Step 6: Scrape Off Excess Wax

Use a plastic scraper to remove excess wax, working from tip to tail.

Step 7: Brush the Base

Use a nylon brush to buff the base and enhance glide performance.
